About

This GPA Calculator was built to make academic tracking simple and stress free. Whether you're checking your standing after exams or planning your semester, just plug in your courses, grades, and credit units -- we handle the math

How it Works

  1. Enter your course name, grade, and credit units for each subject.
  2. Add as many courses as you need using the "Add Course" button
  3. Hit "Calculate GPA" and your result shows up instantly
  4. Print or reset anytime
